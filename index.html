Post-Melee Morale

3-DT Quick Check

A 3-DT is required when charging or breaking off melee if one unit has twice the opponent's Total Morale Value. Pass if any die is equal or under the unit morale.

Dice used: 3 dice (no casualties), 2 dice (casualties), 1 die (poor morale previously).
